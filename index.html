About Us

Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP) is India's largest student organization, committed to empowering students and building a strong nation. We stand for student rights, quality education, and social welfare.

ABVP Tamil Nadu works tirelessly to address student issues, promote educational reforms, and create opportunities for youth leadership. We believe in the power of students to transform society and create a brighter future for all.
